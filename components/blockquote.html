<blockquote>

Blockquotes identifies a section as quoted from another source. It sets apart long, direct quotes from the main text by indentation to visually distinguish text from another source. For short quotes, use <q>.

Here's an example of a blockquote in action:

Linus Torvalds, Just for Fun: The Story of an Accidental Revolutionary
